Who Am I?


As a media enthusiast, I graduated in Bachelor of Mass Communication (Hons) Broadcasting. Over the years, I have built myself as a media professional with 12 years of experience and strong knowledge in production, creative media, communication and branding. I have planned and managed numerous award-winning television shows, documentaries, advertisements and events for notables companies based in Singapore and overseas.

I always visioned myself being a boss in my very own company. So, I levelled up myself into the business world and embarked on the entrepreneurial journey with my life partner. We turned our dream into actions. While the world was fighting against the boredom of Covid in 2020, we fought against all odds and established "The Mighty Bodhie", a digital creative media agency. I'm now the Founder and Director of "The Mighty Bodhie".

I call myself a Philomath for my an unquenchable thirst for knowledge. In today's world, being able to learn from an immense range of sources makes me feel like a kid in a candy store. So, I decided to dive into the tech world and discovered my new love in coding. As a certified Front-End Developer with a spark of creative magic, I thrive to develop an unique and interactive web applications.
